Emotion-Based Questionnaire

1. Your teacher returns your assignment, and you see a note on the cover. How do you feel?

2. You text your best friend, but they don’t reply all day.

3. You prepared a dish for a family dinner, and no one mentioned it during the meal. How do you feel?

4.You forgot to invite a close friend to your birthday party by mistake. How do you feel?

5. You receive a text from a friend you haven’t heard from in a while.

6. You’ve been working hard on a big project at work or school for several weeks. You've put in extra hours, and it's been stressful, but you're confident that your effort will pay off. Today, you finally get the results and the feedback is not what you expected. Your work is criticized heavily, and it feels like your efforts were wasted.

7. You’ve been looking forward to a weekend trip with your friends for months. You’ve made all the plans, booked the accommodations, and everyone was excited. However, just before leaving, you receive a message that the trip has to be canceled because of unexpected circumstances, such as a sudden illness or a personal emergency.

8. You’ve been training for months to run a marathon. The day of the race arrives, and you’ve been feeling nervous, but you push yourself through the entire course. As you cross the finish line, you look at the clock and realize you’ve completed the race in a personal best time. You’ve reached your goal!
